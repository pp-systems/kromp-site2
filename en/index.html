About us

Kromp International is an Austrian company specialising in event and media technology, broadcast engineering and the overall technical planning of complex projects. The company develops, plans and implements high-quality solutions for events, media productions, corporate clients and public institutions.

Core areas include complete technical realisation of events, professional audio, video and lighting systems, broadcast and streaming technology, network infrastructure for events as well as the creation of comprehensive technical concepts as a general planner in the event and media sector.

Kromp International works with international partners, broadcasters and numerous local companies and manages projects of all sizes – from live events and sports broadcasts to complex technical special installations. With precise planning, modern technology and high reliability, Kromp International offers turnkey solutions and sets standards in quality and innovation.
